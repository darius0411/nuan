隐私政策

Privacy Policy · 暖 (Nuan)

暖（以下简称「本应用」）重视您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本政策。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 账号信息：通过 Google 或 Facebook 登录时，我们会获取您的显示名称和电子邮件地址，用于识别您的账号。
  • 用户内容：您在应用内输入的文字（对话内容、昵称、自我介绍）存储在您设备的本地存储中。
  • 使用数据：我们可能收集匿名的应用使用统计数据，用于改善产品体验。

2. 信息的使用方式

  • 提供和改善本应用的功能与体验
  • 识别您的账号，保存您的个人化设置
  • 向您发送关怀提醒（仅在您开启通知权限后）

3. 信息共享

我们不会出售、出租或以商业目的向第三方共享您的个人信息。以下情况除外：

  • Firebase / Google：用于身份验证服务
  • Groq：对话内容会发送至 Groq AI 服务以生成回复，Groq 的隐私政策请参阅 groq.com
  • 法律要求时

4. 数据存储与安全

您的对话记录、昵称、记忆等个人数据主要存储在您设备的本地浏览器存储（localStorage）中，不会上传至我们的服务器。我们采取合理的安全措施保护您的账号信息。

5. 您的权利

  • 您可以随时在应用设置中清除所有本地数据
  • 您可以随时删除您的账号及相关数据
  • 如需删除账号数据，请联系我们（见下方）

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

7. 隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后，我们会在本页面注明最新日期。建议您定期查看本页面。

8. 联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问，或需要删除您的账号数据，请联系：

dariusphh@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

暖 (Nuan) · English

Nuan ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your display name and email address to identify your account.
  • User content: Text you enter in the app (conversations, nickname, self-introduction) is stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's features and experience
  • To identify your account and save your personalized settings
  • To send care reminders (only if you enable notifications)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes, except:

  • Firebase / Google: For authentication services
  • Groq: Conversation content is sent to Groq AI to generate responses. See Groq's Privacy Policy
  • When required by law

4. Data Storage & Security

Your conversations, nickname, memories, and other personal data are primarily stored in your device's local browser storage (localStorage) and are not uploaded to our servers. We take reasonable security measures to protect your account information.

5. Your Rights

  • You can clear all local data at any time in the app settings
  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time
  • To request account data deletion, please contact us (see below)

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy or to request account data deletion, contact us at:

dariusphh@gmail.com
